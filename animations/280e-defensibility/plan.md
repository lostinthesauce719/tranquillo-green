# Tranquillo Green — 280E Defensibility Animation

## Narrative Arc
A cannabis operator receives an IRS notice about 280E compliance. We walk through how Tranquillo Green turns a scary audit moment into a calm, defensible posture — showing every dollar has a story.

## Scenes (6 total, ~90 seconds)

### Scene 1: The Problem (10s)
- Title: "IRC §280E"
- Animate: $100 of expenses → only $40 deductible, $60 "disallowed"
- Red X appears on the disallowed portion
- Subtext: "Cannabis businesses can't deduct ordinary expenses"

### Scene 2: The Allocation (15s)
- Title: "What if you could prove it?"
- Split a bar: 60% COGS (green, deductible) / 40% OpEx (red, 280E-limited)
- Animate allocation methods: Square Footage, Labor Hours, Revenue Mix
- Each method morphs the split ratio

### Scene 3: The Review (15s)
- Title: "Every decision has a story"
- Show an allocation card appearing
- Animate: System Recommendation → 73% deductible
- Tax Manager overrides → 65% deductible
- Reason, evidence, timestamp animate in one by one
- "Who changed it. Why. When."

### Scene 4: The Audit Trail (15s)
- Title: "The audit trail builds itself"
- Timeline appears, events stack up:
  - Override recorded (Tax Manager, Apr 15)
  - Approval (Controller, Apr 16)
  - Policy exception (Controller, Apr 18)
- Each event has a colored dot (amber, green, violet)
- Events connect with lines forming a trail

### Scene 5: The Packet (15s)
- Title: "One click → CPA-ready"
- Documents fly in and assemble into a packet:
  - 280E Support Schedule
  - Override History
  - Policy Memos
  - Reviewer Sign-off
- Packet gets a seal/stamp: "Defensible"

### Scene 6: The Close (10s)
- Title: "Tranquillo Green"
- Tagline: "Obvious. Trustworthy. Defensible."
- Logo animation: leaf icon with subtle glow
- Subtext: "280E defensibility for cannabis operators"
